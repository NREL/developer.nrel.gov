Transportation

Use this Web service documentation to access data about alternative transportation technologies.

ALTRIOS

Provides a select set of features from the ALTRIOS model.

Alternative Fuel Stations

Query our database of alternative fuel stations.

Alternative Fuel Vehicles

Query our database of alternative fuel vehicles, manufacturers, fuels, engines, and hybrid/conversion systems.

Battery Policies and Incentives

Query our database of policies and incentives related to batteries for electric vehicle and stationary energy storage applications

EVI-Pro Lite

APIs providing output from NREL's EVI-Pro model. Used to power the EVI-Pro Lite tool at https://afdc.energy.gov/evi-pro-lite.

RouteE

The Route Energy Prediction Model (RouteE) predicts the energy consumption of a given vehicle over a proposed route.

Transportation Laws and Incentives

Query our database of federal and state laws and incentives for alternative fuels and vehicles, air quality, fuel efficiency, and other transportation-related topics.
